KEVIN SMITH

- Bay Area guitarist and teacher.
Kevin has been a teacher almost as long as he has been a guitar player. From taking on some blues students at the start of his career to the jazz and clasical students of today, he has always been a teacher. He teachs all styles and enjoyed students from 8 to 80.

His teaching philosophy is simple - try to mix the "good" in with the "bad." Too many teachers dwell on the more boring aspects of music and kill the "joy of playing. Conversely, many teacher only focus on the simple and fun aspects of playing - creating a shallow and limited understanding. Kevin tries to find a balence between the two. He tries to spend the first part of the lesson working on fundamentals and the second part working on something fun - a song the student likes, a particular style, etc. The trick is to link them up and show how they relate to each other. That's where the fun and the learning begin!
