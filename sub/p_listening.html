When booking a gig (or simply asking for a quote or for information), it is useful to include the following details:
  • Date and time of the event
  • Nature of the venue (private home, restaurant, wine bar, church, etc.)
  • Duration of the event.
  • The instrumentation in which you are interested (solo jazz, solo classical, jazz duo, trio, quartet, etc.)
  • Any special needs (specific songs, special attire, etc.)
